Gland Packing

High-integrity compression packings designed for extreme temperatures, high shaft speeds, and aggressive chemical environments in process industries globally.

Temp Limit -200°C / +650°C
Pressure Max 300 Bar (Valve)
pH Range 0 — 14 (Inert)
Shaft Speed Up to 25 m/s

Engineered to eliminate leakages under extreme conditions

Our gland packings represent the peak of compression sealing technology. Hand-braided from advanced synthetic yarns, expanded flexible graphite, and custom aramid fibers, these packings provide extremely low frictional resistance while maintaining maximum mechanical structural stability under high valve and pump pressures.

Whether sealing boiler feed pumps, chemical chemical mixers, vacuum reactors, or municipal utility valves, our solutions stand up to corrosive acids, alkaline slurries, organic solvents, and high steam temperatures.

Materials & Braiding Options

⚡ Flexible Graphite Packing: Reinforced with Inconel wire mesh for superheated steam applications.
⚡ Pure PTFE & Carbon Fibers: Inert sealing for critical pharmaceutical and chemical reaction vessels.
⚡ Kevlar / Aramid Corners: Designed to resist abrasive grit, heavy crystals, and sewage slurry.

Need a custom dimension or material braid?

We manufacture custom gland packings cut-to-length, pre-formed rings, or bulk rolls calibrated to your specific industrial pump and valve shaft dimensions.
